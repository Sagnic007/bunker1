Food is a major part of festivities all over the world, but even more so for Bengalis and their festivals. The best part of Bengali festivities is that any other festivals there is no boundation to what one can eat. A huge array of possibilities, from sweet to spicy, veg to non-veg, and authentic to fusion, there are hundreds of options to choose from.We couldnt restrict yourself to the food items and enjoy its goodness in the spirit of the festival.
